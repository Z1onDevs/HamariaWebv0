### 1. **Spanish Capitalization**
Only capitalize:
- First word of sentence/title
- Proper nouns (HydraFacial®, Ultherapy®, etc.)
- Brand names (Signature, Body Lab, etc.)

### 2. **Voice & Tone**
- **Informal "tú"** throughout (tu salud, tu transformación)
- **Present tense** for immediacy
- **Action-oriented** imperatives (Solicita, Únete, Desplázate)

### 3. **Luxury Vocabulary**

- "medicina de precisión" (not "medicina de información")
- "evaluación basal" (not "línea base")

### 5. **Grammar Excellence**
- Proper article usage (la, el, los, las)
- Parallel structure in lists
- Consistent verb forms
- Natural prepositions (a través de, mediante, con, para)
